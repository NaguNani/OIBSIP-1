HIMALAYA COMPANY

Himalaya Wellness Company (formerly Himalaya Drug Company) is an Indian multinational personal care and pharmaceutical company based in Bangalore. It was originally established by Muhammad Manal in Dehradun in 1930. It produces health care products under the name Himalaya Herbal Healthcare whose products include Ayurvedic ingredients. Its operations are spread across locations in India, the United States, the Middle East, Asia, Europe and Oceania, while its products are sold in 106 countries across the world. A hepatic drug, named Liv.52, is its flagship product, first introduced in 1955
